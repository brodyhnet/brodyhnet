    2nd generation tape digitalization



    The original "master" (first generation tape) was transferred onto a 2nd generation tape
    (tape with a recording of the master) to avoid me messing up and damaging the master tape,
    which would've been bad. I also put some info before the video starts.
    some notable differences are the old transfer is in the wrong framerate (30fps converted from 29.97NTSC)
    but this one has some weird frame dropouts. On the start of the old video,
    the PLAY thing is covering part of the screen. It's obviously not in this new one.
    The old transfer has low bitrate, meaning lower digital quality (not a issue here),
    but this new transfer is a generation higher than the old one, so that's lower analog quality.
    There's more detail on this one though.
